About us

Ozzy’s Sports Bar & Grill is a locally owned family friendly sports bar striving to be everything a neighborhood spot should be. Enjoy our delicious homemade food and casual atmosphere while you watch your favorite games or just catch up with friends & family. Whether it's a group gathering, business meeting, dining by the huge open air patio or partying with your friends and family - we do it all! At Ozzy's Roadhouse, we want our place to be there anytime you need it.

Ozzy’s is a great place for families of all ages to enjoy. We believe that our great food and friendly employees lead to the family friendly place you need to get away for a quick lunch or watch your favorite sports with friends!

Our full bar and friendly bartenders are well-equipped to whip up whatever drink you might be craving. We carry a large selection of liquors along with tons of bottled beer and rotating six beers on tap from local craft breweries.

In addition to an extensive menu, including a kids’ menu, the numerous TVs and games (pool table, darts, and arcade games) are aimed to keep everyone having a great time.

Try ozzy’s special Low carb thin crust pizza that can be substituted on any one of our specialty pizzas. If you are in the mood for spicy food, we got you covered too. Try our spicy buffalo wings and spicy hot “ghost pepper cheese” on any pizza, sandwich or burger.

We are a sports bar that caters to any sports fan. We offer most sports packages and channels that DirecTV has to offer and many other specialty sports channels. Even if you're in the mood to catch a different sport, we've got you covered, too. Our lounge and dining room feature 30+ 65" TVs, including a giant 200-inch Projector so that you'll never have a bad seat and never have to miss a second of the game. Have a game you want to see that isn't up on one of our TV screens? We play games on a first-come, first-served basis, so just ask and we'll immediately show it on one of our screens.

Be rest assured that when you come to Ozzy’s, you'll be taken care of. Bring your family and friends down and enjoy sports Ozzy’s way.

Looking forward to sharing some great food and memories

Cheers,
