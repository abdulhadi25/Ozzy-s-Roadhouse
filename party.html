THE BASICS:

  • We offer private parties with a required minimum guest count of 30.
  • To book a party and reserve a specific date/time, you can book through our website /in-person/ phone.
  • Rental Fee is due at the time of booking
  • Monday - Thursday $100
  • Friday - Sunday $200
  • Rental Fee will be credited towards the cost of your food order if you meet the minimum spending limit.
  • A notice of one week is required for all special needs
  • A final confirmed guest count is required seven days prior to the event.
  • Depending on the size of the party up to 20% gratuity charge will be added to your party cost
  • No refunds or credits shall be provided for cancellations made within two weeks.

AMENITIES:

  • Table seating capacity for 80+ people.
  • Four 85’’ HD Projector screens with HDMI ports
  • Private surround sound system and Juke Box
  • Arcade and Board Games
